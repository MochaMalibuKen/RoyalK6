About RK6

Empowering the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers

Our Mission

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower and inspire young individuals to become proactive leaders and compassionate citizens in their communities. We strive to provide a nurturing platform for youth to develop their skills, explore their passions, and engage in meaningful projects that promote social responsibility, innovation, and positive change. Through collaboration, education, and mentorship, we aim to cultivate a generation of informed, resilient, and driven individuals ready to make a lasting impact on the world.

Our Vision

To empower and inspire young individuals to become proactive leaders and change-makers, driving sustainable growth and positive transformation in their communities while fostering a culture of inclusivity, innovation, and resilience.

Our Promise

We promise to provide a nurturing space where every young person feels supported and inspired to reach their full potential. Through our programs, we are committed to making a lasting, positive impact on both the youth we serve and the communities we are a part of.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Community

Building strong, supportive networks that uplift every member.

Innovation

Embracing creativity and new ideas to solve challenges.

Leadership

Developing confident leaders who inspire positive change.

Compassion

Fostering empathy and care for others in all we do.

Meet Our Team

The passionate leaders behind RK6 Youth in Action

KW

Kenya Washington

President & Founder

Kenya Washington is the visionary founder and president of RK6 Youth in Action. With a passion for empowering young people and creating positive change in communities, Kenya has dedicated her life to building platforms that nurture youth leadership and development. Her innovative approach to youth engagement has transformed countless lives and continues to inspire a new generation of leaders.

Under Kenya's leadership, RK6 Youth in Action has grown from a small community initiative to a recognized nonprofit organization making a significant impact across multiple communities. Her commitment to inclusivity, innovation, and sustainable change drives every aspect of the organization's mission.
