Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and organization

How can my child join RK6 programs?

Getting started is easy! Contact us through our volunteer form or give us a call. We'll discuss which programs best fit your child's interests and schedule a meet-and-greet session.

Are there any fees for participation?

Most of our core programs are offered at no cost to participants, thanks to our generous donors and volunteers. Some specialized workshops may have minimal fees to cover materials.

What age groups do you serve?

We primarily serve young people aged 12-24, with specific programs tailored to different age groups and developmental stages. Contact us to learn about age-appropriate opportunities.

Can parents/guardians get involved?

Absolutely! We encourage family involvement and offer various ways for parents and guardians to participate, from volunteering at events to joining our parent advisory committee.
