Program Areas

What Youth Experience With RK6

Each program meets youth where they are and offers safe spaces to grow, try new things, and lead projects that make tangible differences.

Mentorship Program

Young leaders are paired with trained mentors who provide consistent guidance, encouragement, and accountability. Youth gain confidence, communication skills, and a supportive champion in their corner.

Skill Building Workshops

Interactive sessions focus on leadership, teamwork, conflict resolution, entrepreneurship, public speaking, and emotional wellness. Youth leave with practical skills for school and beyond.

Community Service Projects

Service days include neighborhood beautification, supporting shelters, tutoring peers, and health awareness campaigns. These projects cultivate empathy, pride, and civic responsibility.

Creative & Recreational Experiences

Sports clinics, arts intensives, outdoor adventures, and cultural trips help youth explore passions, relieve stress, and form lasting friendships within a nurturing community.
