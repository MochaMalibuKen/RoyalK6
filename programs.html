Our Youth Development Programs

RK6 Youth in Action offers structured programs that support emotional, academic, and leadership growth.

Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program pairs young individuals with experienced mentors who provide guidance, encouragement, and support. Through regular meetings, workshops, and activities, youth build confidence, develop communication skills, gain new perspectives, and receive personalized support toward their goals.

Skill Building Workshops

These workshops equip young people with essential personal and professional skills — including leadership, teamwork, problem-solving, public speaking, and emotional resilience. Youth gain tools they can use in school, future jobs, and everyday life.

Community Service Projects

Youth participate in service opportunities such as clean-up events, volunteering at shelters, tutoring, and health awareness efforts. These projects teach empathy, responsibility, and leadership while strengthening neighborhoods.

Recreational & Creative Activities

From sports leagues and clinics to art, dance, and music workshops, youth explore their passions and build friendships in a supportive environment. Outdoor adventures like camping and hiking help teach resilience, teamwork, and appreciation for nature.
